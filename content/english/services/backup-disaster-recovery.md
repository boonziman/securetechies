## Complete Data Protection Strategy

Data loss doesn't discriminate — it hits businesses of every size, in every industry. At Secure Techies, we build **multi-layered backup and disaster recovery solutions** that protect your business from every angle.

### The 3-2-1 Backup Rule
Every backup strategy we implement follows the industry-standard 3-2-1 rule:

- **3 copies** of your data at all times
- **2 different storage media** (local + cloud)
- **1 offsite copy** for geographic redundancy

This ensures that no single event — fire, flood, ransomware, or hardware failure — can destroy all copies of your critical data.

### What We Back Up
We protect data wherever it lives in your organization:

- Physical and virtual servers (Windows, Linux, VMware, Hyper-V)
- Employee workstations and laptops
- Microsoft 365 (Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive, Teams)
- Google Workspace (Gmail, Drive, Shared Drives)
- SQL databases and line-of-business applications
- SaaS application data (Salesforce, QuickBooks, etc.)

### Ransomware-Proof Backups
Ransomware is the #1 threat to business data. Our approach goes beyond standard backups:

- **Immutable backups** — backup copies that cannot be encrypted, modified, or deleted by ransomware
- **Air-gapped storage** — backup copies stored in isolated environments with no network access
- **Isolated recovery environments** — clean rooms for restoring systems without re-infection
- **Rapid restoration** — get back online in hours, not days or weeks

### Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS)
For businesses that can't afford any downtime, we offer full DRaaS:

- Real-time replication to geographically distributed cloud environments
- Automated failover that activates within minutes
- Regular failover testing with documented results
- Runbook automation for step-by-step recovery procedures
