## Comprehensive Network Security Services

Your network is the backbone of your business — and in today's threat landscape, securing it requires far more than a firewall and a prayer. Secure Techies delivers **multi-layered network security** that protects every connection, every device, and every byte of data flowing through your organization.

### Firewall Management
We partner with the industry's most trusted names to build your perimeter defense:

- **Cisco Meraki** — cloud-managed firewalls ideal for multi-site businesses
- **Fortinet FortiGate** — high-performance next-gen firewalls with advanced threat protection
- **Palo Alto Networks** — enterprise-class firewalls with application-level visibility
- Ongoing rule management, firmware updates, and configuration reviews
- Detailed logging and compliance reporting

### VPN & Remote Access
With hybrid work here to stay, secure remote access is non-negotiable:

- Site-to-site VPN tunnels for multi-office connectivity
- SSL VPN for secure employee remote access
- Always-on VPN configurations for maximum security
- Multi-factor authentication integration
- Split tunneling optimization for performance

### SD-WAN
For businesses with multiple locations, SD-WAN transforms connectivity:

- Intelligent traffic routing across multiple WAN connections
- Application-aware quality of service (QoS)
- Centralized management and visibility across all sites
- Dramatic cost savings over traditional MPLS circuits
- Built-in encryption for all inter-site traffic

### Wireless Network Solutions
Professional WiFi isn't just about coverage — it's about security:

- Comprehensive site surveys for optimal access point placement
- Network segmentation separating corporate, IoT, and guest traffic
- WPA3 Enterprise encryption with 802.1X authentication
- Guest captive portals with bandwidth controls
- Wireless intrusion detection and rogue AP monitoring
