## What's Included in Managed Infrastructure

At Secure Techies, managed infrastructure means we handle **everything** related to your technology environment — so your team can focus on revenue-generating work instead of IT headaches.

### Server Management
Whether you run physical servers on-premise, virtual machines in the cloud, or a hybrid environment, our team manages the full lifecycle:

- Provisioning and initial configuration
- OS patching and security updates on a managed schedule
- Performance monitoring and capacity planning
- Hardware refresh planning and end-of-life management
- Backup verification and disaster recovery integration

### Workstation Lifecycle Management
From the moment a new employee is hired to the day they leave, we manage their technology:

- Device procurement and imaging with your standard build
- Application deployment and ongoing updates
- Security hardening and endpoint protection
- Asset tracking and inventory management
- Secure data wiping and device retirement

### Cloud Services
We help businesses move to — and thrive in — the cloud:

- **Microsoft 365** — deployment, migration, license optimization, and ongoing administration
- **Azure** — virtual machines, storage, networking, and security
- **AWS** — EC2, S3, RDS, and managed services
- Hybrid cloud architectures that balance performance with cost
- Ongoing cost optimization to eliminate cloud waste

### Vendor Management
Stop juggling multiple vendor relationships. We act as your single IT partner and manage:

- ISP and telecom provider relationships
- Hardware warranty claims and RMA processing
- Software licensing renewals and compliance
- Third-party application support coordination
- Pricing negotiations for all technology purchases
