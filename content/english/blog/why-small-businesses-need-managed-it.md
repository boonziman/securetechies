Running a small or mid-size business in Southern California means wearing many hats. Between managing employees, serving clients, and driving growth, IT management often falls to the bottom of the priority list — until something breaks.

## The Real Cost of "Break-Fix" IT

Many small businesses operate on a reactive IT model: wait until something breaks, then call someone to fix it. This approach leads to:

- **Unexpected downtime** that costs an average of $5,600 per minute (according to Gartner)
- **Security vulnerabilities** from outdated systems and unpatched software
- **Lost productivity** as employees struggle with slow or unreliable technology
- **Data loss risks** without proper backup and recovery plans
